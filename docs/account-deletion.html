How to Request Deletion

If you want your MittiMitra account and associated data deleted, send us an email using the button below. Please include the email address or phone number used to create your MittiMitra account so we can identify it.

Request Account Deletion

What We Delete

  • Your Firebase Authentication account.
  • Your profile information, including name, email address, phone number, optional Aadhaar/Kisan details, profile photo, and farm details.
  • Your soil scan history, plant diagnosis history, saved documents, crop schedules, farm tasks, reminders, and chat history stored for your account.
  • Uploaded files or images associated with your account where technically available.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited records only where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup recovery. Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics diagnostics may remain in aggregated or non-identifying form according to Google/Firebase retention periods.

Processing Time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 30 days. If we need more information to confirm account ownership, we will reply to the email address used for the request.

In-App Option

You can also manage or delete app data inside MittiMitra from the account or data management screens when you have access to the app. This web page is provided for users who cannot access the app or have already uninstalled it.
