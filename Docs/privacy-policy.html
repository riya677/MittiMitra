Privacy Policy — Farmer First, Always

Effective Date: 26 April 2026  |  Last Updated: 26 April 2026

MittiMitra ("we", "our", or "the app") is an agricultural assistance application developed as an academic project at Amrita School of Computing. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number you provide during sign-up.
  • Profile Information: Aadhaar number (optional), profile photo, and farm details you choose to add.
  • Location Data: Precise GPS coordinates to fetch local weather, soil data, and nearby mandi prices. Location is used only in the moment it is requested and is not stored on our servers.
  • Camera & Images: Photos taken for soil analysis and plant disease detection. Images are processed locally on your device using an on-device machine learning model. Soil scan results are stored in your Firestore account.
  • Voice Input: Microphone access is used solely for the voice-to-text tip search feature. Audio is processed on-device and is never uploaded or stored.
  • Usage Data: Firebase Analytics collects anonymised usage events (screens viewed, features used) to help us improve the app.
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics collects anonymised crash logs (device model, OS version, stack trace) to help us fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalised soil recommendations, crop calendars, and irrigation advice.
  • Fetch relevant weather alerts and mandi prices for your location.
  • Display your scan history and farm planning data on your device and your linked account.
  • Send optional push notifications for document expiry reminders and mandi price alerts (only if you enable them).
  • Improve app stability and features through anonymised analytics and crash data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Limited data sharing occurs only with:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, storage, analytics, and crash reporting. Governed by Firebase's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo: Your GPS coordinates are sent to fetch weather data. No account is created. See Open-Meteo Terms.
  • ISRIC SoilGrids: Your GPS coordinates are sent to fetch soil nutrient data. No personal data is attached. See ISRIC Data Policy.
  • data.gov.in: Your state/district is used to query mandi prices. No personal data is shared.

4. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Soil scan history and farm plans are retained in your Firestore database until you delete them via the Manage Data screen.
  • Analytics and crash data is retained per Google/Firebase's default retention periods (90 days for Analytics events).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data via the Profile screen.
  • Delete your data using the "Delete Account" option under Account Security. This removes your Firebase Auth account and all associated Firestore data.
  • Export your soil analysis history as CSV from the History screen.

6. Children's Privacy

MittiMitra is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: Firebase App Check (Play Integrity) to verify requests come from legitimate app installs, encrypted SharedPreferences for local token storage, HTTPS for all network communication, and Firestore security rules that restrict each user to their own data only.

8. Sensitive Data (Aadhaar)

Aadhaar number is an optional field stored only in your personal Firestore document, accessible only to you. It is never logged, never sent to third parties, and is masked in the UI after entry. We recommend not entering this unless required for a specific government scheme you are applying for within the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notice. The "Last Updated" date at the top will always reflect the current version.
